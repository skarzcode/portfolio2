Process

competitor analysis

I didn't have any design given to me so my firt task was to look at other property developers in london to get some inspiration for color schemes, content structure and site functionalities

Figma Design

Once i had a few ideas in my head i went straight to the drawing board to make some mockups. I used Figma as it was easy and quick to learn and with minimal effort and I made three different Designs to showcase to my clients and get some feedback.

Dynamic Version

Finally after creating and showcasing my design mockups i began writing the HTML and CSS for te site and after each section was completed i relayed with the client to get their feedback to see whether is was inline with what they wanted.
